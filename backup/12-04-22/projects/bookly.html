Booky: Streaming platform.

Is an app designed for students, the most of the times studets doen’t found the books they need in college libraries or sometimes they are out of stock and buy the books is too much expensive to them.

Bookly is designed to find every college book in one place. Students can access to this content paying a monthly suscription, but they can read all books they need.

This app also have in each book a Q&A section for get answers from other students or find more information about complicate subjects.
