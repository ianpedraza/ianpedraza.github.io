Jabflash

Allows mainteniment and software fix to Android devices. You can make backups, restore signal when is lost, install the whole SO when device fails, remove the passcode when you forget it, root, reset the device, turn it off, etc.

Even has an emergency mode when devices doesn’t respond anymore, you can restore the whole software to bring it to life again.

Jabflash support many android smartphones models, like Motorola, Samsung, Pixel, Nexus, and universal Android devices.
