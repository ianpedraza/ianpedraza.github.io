Panda AR

As all of us know pandas are in danger of extinction. Using technologies like AR or VR we can reach to many people to test this app and let them know a little bit more about pandas.

Across this app you can play in AR with pandas, know about their habitat, feeding, etc. You also can watch videos in VR about them and the cares of panda shelter.

So if we mix new technologies for getting people attention just a second, we can inform people quickly about what’s happening while they're playing and exploring this new technologies.
